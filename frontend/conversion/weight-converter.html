Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between US ton and UK ton?

A US ton (also called short ton) equals 2,000 pounds or 907.185 kg. A UK ton (also called Imperial ton or long ton) equals 2,240 pounds or 1,016.05 kg. The UK ton is about 12% heavier than the US ton.

How many grams in a pound?

1 pound equals exactly 453.592 grams. This is a defined conversion factor.

What is a stone in weight?

A stone is a British unit of weight equal to 14 pounds or about 6.35 kilograms. It's still commonly used for measuring body weight in the United Kingdom and Ireland.

Is weight the same as mass?

Technically, mass is the amount of matter in an object (measured in kg), while weight is the force of gravity on that mass (measured in Newtons). However, for everyday purposes on Earth, we use these terms interchangeably. Our converter handles both.
