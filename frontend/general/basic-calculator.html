Frequently Asked Questions

Does it follow order of operations (PEMDAS)?

Yes! The calculator follows standard mathematical order of operations (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication/Division, Addition/Subtraction).

Can I use my keyboard?

Absolutely! All number keys (0-9) and operation keys (+, -, *, /) work. Press Enter for equals, Escape to clear, and Backspace to delete.

What are the memory functions?

MC (Memory Clear) clears memory, MR (Memory Recall) shows stored value, M+ adds to memory, M- subtracts from memory. Great for running totals!

Is there a calculation limit?

The calculator can handle very large numbers (up to JavaScript's Number.MAX_SAFE_INTEGER, about 9 quadrillion).
