FAQ

Does it handle order of operations (PEMDAS)?

Yes, the calculator follows standard mathematical order of operations.

Can I use my keyboard?

Yes! All number and operator keys are supported. Use 'Enter' for equals and 'Escape' to clear.

What are the memory functions?

M+ (add to memory), M- (subtract from memory), MR (recall memory), and MC (clear memory).
