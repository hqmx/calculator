Concrete Calculator Guide

Formula

Volume (m³) = Length × Width × Thickness × (1 + Wastage%)

Concrete Mix Ratios

  • 1:2:3 - General purpose (1 cement : 2 sand : 3 gravel)
  • 1:2:4 - Foundations and footings
  • 1:1.5:3 - High strength (columns, beams)

Typical Thicknesses

  • 10 cm: Pathways, light slabs
  • 15 cm: Driveways, garage floors
  • 20-30 cm: Heavy-duty slabs
  • 30+ cm: Foundations, footings
