Wallpaper Calculator Guide

Standard Roll Sizes

  • European: 53 cm × 10 m (5.3 m²)
  • American: 20.5 in × 33 ft (~5.2 m²)

Tips

  • Always buy 1-2 extra rolls for mistakes and future repairs
  • Patterns with large repeats need more wallpaper
  • Deduct area for large windows and doors
  • Buy from same batch for color consistency
