Understanding PPI & DPI

PPI (Pixels Per Inch)

Measures pixel density of a screen. Higher PPI = sharper display.

PPI = √(width² + height²) / diagonal

Common Resolutions

  • Full HD: 1920 × 1080 (2.1 MP)
  • 2K: 2560 × 1440 (3.7 MP)
  • 4K UHD: 3840 × 2160 (8.3 MP)
  • 8K UHD: 7680 × 4320 (33.2 MP)
