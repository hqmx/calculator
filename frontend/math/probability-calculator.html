Understanding Probability

Combination (nCr)

Number of ways to choose r items from n items where order doesn't matter.

nCr = n! / (r! × (n-r)!)

Permutation (nPr)

Number of ways to arrange r items from n items where order matters.

nPr = n! / (n-r)!
