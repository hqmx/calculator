Understanding Statistics

Key Statistical Measures

  • Mean: Average of all values (sum √∑ count)
  • Median: Middle value when sorted
  • Mode: Most frequently occurring value
  • Standard Deviation: Measure of data spread
  • Variance: Square of standard deviation

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between mean and median?

Mean is the average (sum/count), while median is the middle value. Median is less affected by outliers.

What does standard deviation tell us?

Standard deviation measures how spread out the data is. Lower values mean data is closer to the mean.
